Benjamin's Data Analytics Projects

This contains my project on recruitment analysis using power bi. The project was aimed at optimizing recruitment operations as well as significantly reducing hiring cost for the HR department.

Employee Performace Analysis by Recruitment Source

Here, the average performance rating of employees from various employment sources where highlighted and gauged against the company's average. This was also measured by age-range, marital status and whether employment method was internal or external. The insights generated where succint in the pointing to the most productive employee sources and reliable recruitment pipelines. Productivity by age-range and marital status where clear to see for informed decision making.

RECRUITMENT AND EMPLOYEE COST ANALYSIS

In this analysis, we highlighted the various cost items connected to recruitment exercise. And we also considered the cost implication in terms of employee hourly pay-rate demands and gauged it against recruitment sources, age-range, marital status as well as whether internally or externally hired. These provided great insights as to the most fitting areas to look towards, to achieve cost reduction .
